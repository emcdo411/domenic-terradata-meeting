# Teradata Executive Brief
### Governance Accountability Gap

![Audience](https://img.shields.io/badge/Audience-Executive-black)
![Focus](https://img.shields.io/badge/Focus-Governance-red)

---

## Core Finding

Teradata provides platform-level governance  
but does not establish accountability ownership

---

## Why This Matters

Emerging regulations require:

- Named decision owner
- Post-deployment accountability
- Defensible governance structure

---

## Current State

- Guardrails implemented
- Automation present
- Ownership undefined

---

## Risk

- Compliance exposure
- Decision ambiguity
- Drift without accountability

---

## Reframe

> Governance is not tooling  
> Governance is ownership

---

## Strategic Opportunity

Establish a governance layer that includes:

- Named operations sponsor
- Feedback loop ownership
- Decision accountability structure

---

## Engagement Entry Point

The gap between:

- Platform capability
- Organizational accountability

---

## Closing

The companies that solve this gap first  
will define the next phase of enterprise AI adoption
