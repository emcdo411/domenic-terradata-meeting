The DARE Diagnostic

DARE functions as a decision-compression layer. It forces the conversation away from broad AI excitement and into a clear operating question: where is accountability owned after AI is deployed?

D

Data

Tests whether insight, telemetry, and agent behavior are converted into governed operational action rather than left as passive platform evidence.

A

Agility

Tests whether governance assumptions expire fast enough to keep pace with autonomous AI, regulatory change, and client-side operating complexity.

R

Risks

Tests whether compliance language is being used as cover for missing ownership, stale criteria, or unresolved post-deployment responsibility.

E

Evolution

Tests whether leadership is building toward future accountability requirements or relying on prior-cycle platform comfort.

SHT Scorecard

The SHT read shows a structural tension: platform capability is credible, but regulatory independence and narrative transparency sit below the extraction signal threshold.

Core Finding

Public positioning supports Teradata's platform governance capability. It does not publicly confirm a required client-side operations sponsor embedded in the engagement model.

That distinction matters because the regulatory direction of travel requires named human accountability, not only automated controls or model guardrails.

Conversation Hook

The gap I am seeing is not whether Teradata can govern models at the platform layer. The gap is whether the client has a named operations sponsor responsible for closing the post-deployment accountability loop.

Evidence Delta

The brief separates claimed capability from verifiable evidence and required accountability. That delta is the advisory opening.

Claimed or Visible

Guardrails, evaluations, compliance checks, platform governance language, human-in-the-loop language, and autonomous AI deployment positioning.

→

Missing or Unconfirmed

Required client-side operations sponsor, explicit Measure-to-Manage owner, named post-deployment review loop, and accountable drift response mechanism.
